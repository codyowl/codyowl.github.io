Privacy Policy for WriterPad

Effective Date: July 12, 2026

WriterPad is designed to help users organize notes, ideas, documents, and visual node maps.

Your privacy is important. WriterPad does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

WriterPad does not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your contacts
  • Your location
  • Your photos
  • Your browsing activity
  • Your notes, documents, workspaces, or node content
  • Analytics or advertising identifiers

Local Data Storage

The notes, workspaces, documents, and nodes you create in WriterPad are stored locally on your device. WriterPad does not upload your content to our servers.

In-App Purchases

WriterPad may offer a Pro unlock through Apple In-App Purchase. Purchases are processed securely by Apple. WriterPad does not receive or store your payment card details.

Tracking and Advertising

WriterPad does not show third-party ads and does not track you across apps or websites.

Children's Privacy

WriterPad is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

WriterPad uses Apple services such as the App Store and StoreKit for app distribution and in-app purchases. Apple's handling of data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
